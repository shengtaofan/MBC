About Us

Master Builder Consulting provides 1.6 million Taiwanese and Chinese small and medium-sized enterprises with consulting services for organizational development such as digital transformation, intergenerational prosperity, and interdisciplinary innovation, through performing arts and systemic constellations.

Our Team

Publication Resources

Performing Arts Facilitation Coaching and Business Organizational Systemic Constellations is authored by Dr. Sheng-Tao Fan. It is the world’s first book discussing business organizational systemic constellations from the perspective of performing arts.

Systemic Constellations

Business Organizational Systemic Constellations is a management consulting method. It can quickly, effectively, and deeply find out the root causes of business issues and propose winning strategies. It is originated in Germany and has been popular in Europe for more than 20 years. It is used by world-renowned companies such as BMW, Siemens, and Microsoft. It is introduced to Taiwan by JAASCOB in 2018. Master Builder Consulting Company Limited starts commercial application in 2023. We provide business owners, mid-level managers, and senior executives with digital transformation, intergenerational prosperity, interdisciplinary innovation, as well as leadership and organizational development consulting services.

Theoretical Foundations

Economics
Unlike tedious and time-consuming analytical reports, we utilize the simulation approach of economics to present real-world scenarios, enabling business owners to immediately visualize a clear roadmap and enhance decision-making efficiency.
Drama
Going beyond the operational aspects and disengaging from complex dynamics, we apply the aesthetic distance to position business owners at a vantage point, allowing them to grasp the complete business landscape and achieve forward-looking leadership.
Psychology
Diverging from traditional mechanistic and localized management techniques, we employ a systemic perspective from psychology to provide business owners with a more comprehensive and macro-level strategic approach, assisting them in addressing the ever-changing market challenges.
